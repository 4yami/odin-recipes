Recipe for Garlic Bread

Desciption

Its bread with garlic and butter. Simple yet delcious way to start you day

Ingredients

  • A loaf of bread
  • 2 tabble spoon of Butter
  • 4 cloves of crushed garlic
  • Love

Steps

  1. Heat the garlic and bread in microwave for 1 minutes
  2. Toast the bread until golden brown
  3. Spread the garlic butter on top of the bread
  4. Enjoy the dish
